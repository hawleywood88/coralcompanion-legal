Delete Your Account

Coral Companion

You can permanently delete your Coral Companion account and all associated data directly from the app:

  1. Open Coral Companion and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the warning, then type DELETE to permanently remove your account.

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, island progress, museum and altar records, townsfolk relationships, and any feedback you've submitted tied to your account. This cannot be undone.

If you're unable to access the app, email support@coralcompanion.app from the email address associated with your account and we'll delete it for you.
