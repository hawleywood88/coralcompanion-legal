Privacy Policy

Coral Companion · Last updated July 1, 2026

Coral Companion is an unofficial fan-made companion app for the game Coral Island. It is developed and operated by Shane Gragg ("I", "me", "my"). This policy explains what information I collect, how I use it, and your choices.

By using Coral Companion you agree to this policy. If you don't agree, please don't use the app.

1. Information I Collect

Account information

If you choose to sign in, you sign in through Apple or Google. I receive your email address and, if provided by Apple, your name. I do not create or store passwords — authentication is handled entirely by Apple and Google.

Display name

After signing in, you're asked to choose a display name. This is the only name stored in association with your account.

Game progress

If you're signed in, the following progress data is synced to the cloud so you can access it across devices:

  • Museum donations (item IDs you've marked as donated)
  • Altar offerings (offering IDs you've marked as complete)
  • Items you've found and shipped
  • NPC relationship levels and gift history
  • Island profile names

If you use the app as a guest, this data is stored only on your device and is never sent to me.

Purchase information

If you buy Coral Plus, the purchase is processed by the Apple App Store. To validate the purchase and unlock Coral Plus across your devices, I use RevenueCat, which receives an anonymized user identifier and your App Store purchase receipt. I never receive or store your payment card details.

Feedback

If you submit feedback through the app, I collect your message, your app version, and your device platform (iOS or Android). If you're signed in, your user ID is attached so I can follow up if needed. Feedback from guests is stored anonymously.

Usage analytics

I use PostHog to collect anonymous usage data — things like which screens are viewed, which features are used, and how often. This helps me understand how the app is being used and where to focus improvements. PostHog does not receive your name, email, or any personally identifiable information unless you are signed in, in which case an anonymous user ID is used to associate events with a single user (not a real identity).

What I do not collect

I do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or microphone input. I do not use advertising networks. I do not track you across other apps or websites.

2. How I Use Your Information

  • To sync your game progress across your devices
  • To display your display name within the app
  • To validate your Coral Plus purchase and unlock it across your devices
  • To read and respond to feedback you submit
  • To operate and improve the app

I do not sell your data. I do not use your data for advertising. I do not share your data with any third parties except as described in Section 3.

3. Third Parties

Supabase. I use Supabase (supabase.com) to store account information and synced game progress. Supabase acts as a data processor on my behalf and is subject to its own privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

RevenueCat. I use RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) to validate in-app purchases and manage your Coral Plus entitlement across devices. RevenueCat receives an anonymized user identifier and your App Store purchase receipt. Its privacy policy is at revenuecat.com/privacy.

PostHog. I use PostHog (posthog.com) for product analytics. PostHog receives anonymous usage events (screens viewed, features used) to help me improve the app. No personally identifiable information is sent. PostHog's privacy policy is at posthog.com/privacy.

Sign in with Apple. Apple handles authentication when you choose to sign in with Apple. Apple's privacy policy is at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Sign in with Google. Google handles authentication when you choose to sign in with Google. Google's privacy policy is at policies.google.com/privacy.

No other third parties receive your data.

4. Data Retention

Your account data and game progress are retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data — including your profile, island progress, NPC relationship data, and islands — is permanently deleted. Feedback you submitted is anonymized (your user ID is removed) but the message content is retained so I can track recurring issues.

Guest data exists only on your device and is removed when you clear the app's local data or uninstall the app.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Guest mode. You can use the app without creating an account. No data is sent to me in guest mode.

Delete your account. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm by typing "DELETE". Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Clear local data. You can clear all locally cached data through Settings → Danger Zone → Clear Local Data.

Data requests. To request a copy of your data or ask questions about what I store, email me at support@coralcompanion.app.

6. Children

Coral Companion is not directed at children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information to me, please contact me at support@coralcompanion.app and I will delete it promptly.

7. Security

Authentication is handled by Apple and Google — I never see or store passwords. Your synced data is stored in Supabase with row-level security, meaning your data is only accessible by you. No security system is perfect, but I take reasonable steps to protect your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

If I make material changes to this policy, I'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
